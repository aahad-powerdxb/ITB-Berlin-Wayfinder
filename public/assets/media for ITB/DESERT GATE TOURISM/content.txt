Desert Gate Tourism began as a visionary dream in 2004, founded on professionalism, deep industry expertise, and a passion for creating unforgettable guest experiences. From a ground-handling service for tour operators, we have grown into a premier Destination Management Company across six destinations: UAE, Oman, Qatar, Maldives, Mauritius, and Sri Lanka. Our portfolio features bespoke hotel bookings, elevated transfers, curated excursions, MICE services, and sports experiences. At Desert Gate Tourism, we deliver experiences that go beyond the ordinary, connect people, and transform journeys.
