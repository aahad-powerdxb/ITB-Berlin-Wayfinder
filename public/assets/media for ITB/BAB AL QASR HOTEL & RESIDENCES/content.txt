BAB AL QASR, meaning “door of the palace,” reflects its heritage through a striking Arabic doorway and gleaming copper-toned architecture. Inspired by Arabian culture, the hotel features Bedouin-style accents, Islamic geometric patterns, and a palm-filled lobby with a falaj-inspired water feature. This five-star hotel is located on the west end of Abu Dhabi and opened in late 2016. It is 38 km from Abu Dhabi International Airport and 90 minutes from Dubai. Bab Al Qasr offers 412 hotel rooms and suites and 265 serviced residences with luxurious modern design and stunning views of the Presidential Palace, Corniche, and Arabian Gulf.
