From sunrise treats to sunset rituals, discover an electrifying island escape at W Abu Dhabi – Yas Island, the world’s only hotel built on top of a legendary racetrack. Featuring 499 stylish rooms and suites with stunning track and marina views, every stay is designed to thrill. Indulge in bold flavours at Garage, Brooklyn Chop House and Pappas Taverna, sip handcrafted cocktails at W Lounge, unwind at WET Deck or recharge at AWAY Spa. With vibrant event spaces, FIT gym access and Yas Island’s iconic theme parks just minutes away, this is luxury with an edge.
