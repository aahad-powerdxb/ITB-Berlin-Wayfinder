Abu Dhabi Royal Equestrian Arts (ADREA) is the world’s fifth school of classical horsemanship and the first to rise beyond Europe. Rooted in the Arab world’s centuries-old tradition of Furusiyya, ADREA represents heritage, discipline and artistry.  At its heart are equestrian performances, the preservation of knowledge and immersive learning experiences that reflect the bond between horse and rider.  ADREA offers programmes from foundational training to advanced degrees in classical horsemanship. It is home to the Furusiyya Gallery, an all-equestrian Library, a saddle-making atelier and more.
