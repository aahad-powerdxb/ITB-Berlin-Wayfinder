Tourism365, the tourism arm of ADNEC Group, leads Abu Dhabi’s tourism industry by creating unforgettable travel experiences. With a vision to be a top travel organization in the MENA region, we connect travelers with unique destinations. Operating as a Destination Management Company (DMC) in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Oman, and Jordan, we also offer corporate travel solutions through our Travel Management Company (TMC). Our tour operator brands, Capital Holidays and Etihad Holidays, serve clients in the UAE, UK, and Germany, catering to a wide range of travel needs, from budget to luxury.
