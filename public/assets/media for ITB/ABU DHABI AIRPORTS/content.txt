Zayed International Airport (AUH), is the airport of the future, poised to welcome up to 45 million passengers annually and up to 11,000 passengers per hour, with a world first, nine biometric touchpoints by 2025, a journey that is set to transform the way we travel, forever.  The airport houses one of the largest environmental art pieces in the region, emphasizing the importance of passenger well-being, speed of kerb-to-gate journey and an array of retail, F&B and in-airport hotel options that ensures passengers travel relaxed and rejuvenated.
