In the heart of the UAE, Abu Dhabi’s Al Ain Region offers a journey through time, where heritage meets nature. Under the Department of Culture and Tourism – Abu Dhabi, Experience Al Ain showcases UNESCO-listed oases, majestic forts, museums, and vibrant local culture. Visitors are invited to discover authentic Emirati traditions, adventure, and warm hospitality within the UAE’s cultural heartland.  Al Dhafra Region is the vast western expanse within the Emirate of Abu Dhabi where life unfolds across open desert, quiet coastlines and landscapes shaped by nature and tradition. As the largest region in the emirate, it offers unhurried exploration and diverse experiences—from towering sand dunes in the Liwa Desert and wildlife encounters on Sir Bani Yas Island to historic forts and tranquil islands. The region’s living heritage and relaxed pace make it a unique destination for those seeking space, calm, and new discoveries beyond the city.
