Conrad Abu Dhabi Etihad Towers is a luxury urban resort and culinary destination, home to over 12 world-class dining venues, a luxurious spa with 13 treatment rooms, a private beach, three swimming pools, and the Observation Deck at 300, offering breathtaking 360-degree views of the city from the 74th floor. Stay inspired in its 576 elegant rooms, suites, and residences, all with spectacular views of the Arabian Gulf. The resort is just minutes from the Presidential Palace, setting the standard for luxury hospitality and extraordinary experiences.
